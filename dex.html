Our Menu

Dough's menu is carefully curated to bring customers a selection of flavors and textures to satisfy different tastes and cravings.


Our doughnuts are made with a brioche-type dough prepared in small batches throughout the day so our customers can always get the freshest doughnuts possible! We offer many different flavors and try to keep our most popular available year round while others rotate and change seasonally.


While we are working on bringing lots of new types of doughnuts including cake doughnuts and churros, we invite you to try our deliciously addictive Doughkas as the newest addition to our menu. Inspired by the Babka, twisted yeast loaves of Eastern European Jewish tradition, Doughkas are made with our signature doughnut dough. For now, the Doughkas are only available at our Flatiron location but can be ordered in advance for pick up at our Bed Stuy location as well.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell by the dozen?

We offer several assorted boxes: the Tier 1 Dozen, an assortment of classics such as Raspberry Sprinkle ⓥ, Vanilla Bean ⓥ, and Salted Caramel Cake, as well as our Mixed Tier Dozen, which includes doughnuts from tiers 1 to 3, such as Orange Pistachio ⓥ, Jam Filled Rounds ⓥ, and Bacon Caramel.

In addition, we have a "build-your-own" option with $ 1 off at each half-dozen and $4 at each dozen.

Can I preorder?

Yes! Orders may be placed at least 24 hours in advance by calling (470) 428-2023 during business hours. You may also place your preorder in the shop by using our pre-order worksheet.

If you're interested in pre-assorted boxes and/or coffee boxes, you may also order in our online store. (Online store orders are available for pickup from our Inman Park store only. All other orders must be made over the phone or in person at the desired location). All pre-orders must be paid at the time they are placed, unless other terms are agreed upon by the store manager.

See the question to the right for more information on larger orders.

Can I order in bulk for a wedding, baby shower, or party?

We'd love to be at your big event! In addition to standard doughnuts, we offer mini doughnuts and doughnut cakes, coffee boxes (including cups, sugars, etc), and chai boxes. For those ordering less than 10 dozen standard doughnuts, call (470) 428-2023 at least 24 hours before pickup to place your order. For those ordering large orders or those with special requests, please fill out our special orders form.

DO YOU USE SOY? PEANUTS? CORN? ARTIFICIAL COLORS??

While we do not use soy, peanuts, or corn in any of our base doughs, they are occasionally used in our glazes and fills. Therefore, we can not guarantee that our products are absolutely free of any of these ingredients. While we offer some options for folks with dietary restrictions, we can not guarantee that our products are completely free of bacon, nuts, gluten, eggs, or other allergens as we use these products regularly in the kitchen.

DO YOU DELIVER?

We do not offer delivery at this time. Various delivery services offer our doughnuts but we do not partner with these services and their menus may not reflect what we are currently offering.“

WHAT DOES "VEGAN" Ⓥ MEAN?

Vegan” means made without ingredients sourced from animals (dairy, meat, eggs, honey). Many of our doughnuts are vegan (about 85% of them) - however, we are not an exclusively vegan bakery and our treats may not be suitable for folks who, for ethical reasons, choose not to eat products which may have come in contact with animal products.

To find our vegan treats, look for the ⓥ indicator on our menu board and case labels.

DO YOU SELL GIFT CARDS?

Yes, we sell gift cards at our locations as well as online e-gift cards. You can use our gift cards at all of our locations, including our farmers market booths.

DO YOU PROVIDE NUTRITION INFORMATION / LABELS?

We take pride in having developed all our own recipes and are particular about choosing high quality ingredients in the production of our treats. As a small scale bakery serving ready-to-eat products, we are not required by the FDA to undertake the expense of developing nutrition labels for our products. Our bakers and baristas are happy to answer basic questions about ingredients in our scratch-made products so that folks with food allergies and dietary restrictions are able to select products to fit their particular needs.

DO YOU MAKE GLUTEN FREE DOUGHNUTS?

Yes! Well almost. We offer a baked cake doughnut made from almond/coconut flour which might be suitable for those following a gluten-free diet. We can not guarantee that these doughnuts are free of trace amounts of gluten as they are made in a facility where these products are present.
